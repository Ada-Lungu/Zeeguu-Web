We transform the web into your language playground!


Every unknown word is an opportunity.

Read Anything

You read the texts that you like. When you don't know a word, right click on it and select Translate from the menu. The word, translation, and context are uploaded automatically to your online profile.

Review Anywhere

The online profile with all the words that you are learning is available on all your devices. You can rehearse your words when you are commuting by train, or even while waiting for your friend who is late again... Ah, why isn't everybody swiss?

Rehearse Adaptively

Zeeguu uses intelligent algorithms to personally train you based on relative word importance, the context in which you encountered words, and the last time you saw a word (this is called, spaced repetition).

How does it work?

You translate words everywhere. Zeeguu learns about what you know, and what you don't know. You get intelligent reminders and progress feedback as your vocabulary improves.
