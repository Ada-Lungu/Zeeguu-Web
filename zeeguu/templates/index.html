The web is your textbook


Wtih Zeeguu you enrich your vocabulary seamlessly while reading content that is interesting to you as opposed to reading boring texts from language textbooks which always talk about Maria who is a baby-sitter in Spain.

How does it work?

You read the texts you like on the web

With Zeeguu the entire Web is your textbook and you can read any text you like since the awesome Zeeguu plugin shows you the translations of unknown words with your favorite dictionary.

Zeeguu learns about the words you do not know

You do not waste time writing words down or thinking which to learn first: Zeeguu takes care. The Chrome plugin keeps track of the words you do not know and and the context in which you encounter them.

Your vocabulary improves with personalized exercises

Based on the words you do not know, the time you last saw them, the context where you encountered them, and a bit of Zeeguu magic you are presented with personalized exercises at the Language Gym.
