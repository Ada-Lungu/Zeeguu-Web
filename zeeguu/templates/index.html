The web is your textbook


Encountering an unknown word in the language you are learning is an opportunity. Zeeguu helps you take advantage.

Using Zeeguu and reading the text you like might be even more entertaining and efficient than reading textbook texts talking about Maria who is a baby-sitter in Cologne.

How does it work?

Read Anything

You read the texts that you like and are fit for your level. When you don't know a word the Zeeguu plugin translates it for you. If you want to learn a word, you upload it with one click to your profile.

Review Anywhere

The profile with all the words you are learning is available on all your devices. You can rehearse your words when you are commuting by train, or even while waiting for your friend who is late again.

Rehearse Adaptively

The Language Gym has intelligent algorithms that will train you based on relative word importance, the context in which you encountered words, and the last time you saw a word (spaced repetition).
